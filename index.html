LIVING AROUND
1 of 2

My main interests are focused on several aspects of Evolutionary Biology and Ecology, with reptiles as model species. Using my background on phylogeography, I run several projects on determining the geographical origin of many introduced reptile populations. With the use of metabarcoding I am investigating the diet of these introduced populations and their impact on native taxa. Also, with the latest genomic tools I want to understand the adaptations behind some of the successfully introduced populations.
